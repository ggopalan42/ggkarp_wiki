# JEPA: Non-Generative Predictive World Models (LeCun et al.)

The counter-philosophy to reconstruction- and generation-based world models: predict in an abstract embedding space rather than pixel space.

- **Yann LeCun, "A Path Towards Autonomous Machine Intelligence" (2022)** — the JEPA manifesto. Argues for energy-based, non-generative predictive models. Essential for understanding the AMI Labs / JEPA direction.
- **V-JEPA and V-JEPA 2** (Meta, 2024-2025) — practical instantiations of that vision for video.
- **Fei-Fei Li's 2025 essay** on a "taxonomy of world models" — a conceptual map of competing definitions (not a technical paper).

- **Framework placement:** see [[four-axis-reading-framework]] — abstract embedding representation, self-supervised prediction, non-generative (no reconstruction).
