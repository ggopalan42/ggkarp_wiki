# World Models (Ha & Schmidhuber, 2018)

The canonical modern paper and the clearest single on-ramp to the field. Recommended first read.

- **Architecture:** VAE (vision) + MDN-RNN (memory/dynamics) + linear controller.
- **Key result:** trains an agent that can learn to drive and play games *inside its own hallucinated dream* of the environment.
- **Why start here:** well-written, with an interactive companion site (worldmodels.github.io).
- **Framework placement:** see [[four-axis-reading-framework]] — reconstruction representation, RNN dynamics, policy/planning in imagination, reconstruction supervision.

Status: not yet ingested as a primary source; summary above is from the roadmap. Read alongside the interactive site.
