# MuZero (Schrittwieser et al., 2019/2020)

"Mastering Atari, Go, Chess and Shogi by Planning with a Learned Model" (DeepMind).

- **Idea:** learns a model only of the reward/value/policy-relevant dynamics — no need to reconstruct observations — and plans with MCTS.
- **Why it matters:** a different philosophy from Dreamer (value-equivalent rather than reconstruction-based); worth contrasting directly.
- **Framework placement:** see [[four-axis-reading-framework]] — value-relevant representation, learned latent dynamics, planning (MCTS), reward/value supervision.

Status: not yet ingested as a primary source; summary above is from the roadmap.
